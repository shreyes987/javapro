SYMBOLS



AND GATE SYMBOLS
Each one of these statements stand for
an "AND" equation. The upside down
V is a symbol for "AND"
as well as PQ means P AND Q.
OR GATE SYMBOLS
Each one of these statements stand for
an "OR" equation. The V and + are
symbols for "OR".
NOT (INVERTER) SYMBOLS
The P' symbolizes an inverter or "NOT".
Each output will be the complement (opposite)
of it's input. The same thing for the (Not P).
