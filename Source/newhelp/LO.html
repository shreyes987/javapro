LOGIX OVERVIEW

Logix is a software designed to fulfill basic needs for designing digital logic.Logix gives you the following:

  1. Reducer:'Reducer' helps in reducing and standardising the boolean equations within a click of the mouse.'Reducer' uses an optimized version of the QuineMcCluskey algorithm used for reducing equations.'Reducer ' is designed for reducing only 'Sum Of Products'(SOP) terms.


  2. Circuit Designer:'Circuit Designer' helps in designing circuits with the help of 'And','Or' and 'Not' gates.You can provide input to the circuit and within a click of mouse,get the output. 'Circuit Designer' helps in building memory circuits,complex circuits etc.
