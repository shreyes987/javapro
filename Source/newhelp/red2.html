Steps to reduce the entered equation

  1. Enter the boolean equation in 'Enter your equation' textbox.
  2. Click on 'Reduce' button as shown in fig(2).
  3. The reduced equation will be displayed in 'Reduced Equation' textbox.
  4. Number of variables in the entered equation will be displayed in 'No. of variables' textbox.
