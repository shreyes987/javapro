Steps to standardise the entered equation

  1. Enter the boolean equation in 'Enter your equation' textbox.
  2. Click on 'Standardise' button as shown in fig(2).
  3. The standardised equation will be displayed in 'Standardised form' textbox.
  4. Number of terms in the standardised equation will be displayed in 'No. of terms' textbox.
