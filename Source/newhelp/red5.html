RETRIEVING PREVIOUS ENTERED BOOLEAN EQUATION

Steps to retrieve previous entered equation

  1. For getting the previous entered equation ,click on 'Previous' button as shown in fig(1)
  2. The previous entered equation will be displayed in 'Enter your equation' textbox.
  3. For reducing and standarising the equation,click on 'Reduce' and 'Standardise' button respectively.
